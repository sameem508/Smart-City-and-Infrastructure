ABOUT LUDHIANA

RANK NUMBER 1

Ludhiana has been ranked number one in Area and Population in Punjab. Ludhiana has been ranked number one in milk production in Punjab

TITLE

It is a major industrial center of northern India, referred to as the India's Manchester by BBC.
.

EDUCATION

Ludhiana has 363 senior secondary, 367 high, 324 middle, 1129 primary, and pre-primary Schools, with a total of 398,770 students

AGRICULTURE

The largest area is under wheat crop. Other important crops of the state are rice, cotton, sugarcane, pearl millet, maize, barley and fruit.

ECONOMICAL HUB

The World Bank ranked Ludhiana as the city in India with the best business environment in 2009 and 2013.

INDUSTRIAL HUB

Ludhiana is Punjab, India city's and largest industrial hub. It is known for hosiery and bicycle manufacturing
